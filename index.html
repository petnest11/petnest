Pet Care Tips

Daily Exercise

Most pets need at least 30 minutes of active play or walks every day to stay healthy.

Regular Vet Visits

Schedule annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental cleanings for long-term health.

Balanced Diet

Choose age-appropriate, vet-approved food and avoid feeding human junk food.

Love & Training

Positive reinforcement, patience, and affection help your pet feel safe and confident.
